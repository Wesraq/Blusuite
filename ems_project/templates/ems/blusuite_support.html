Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I import existing employee data?

Yes, you can import employee data using our CSV import tool. Go to Settings > Import Data.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, mobile money, and bank transfers.

How do I upgrade my subscription plan?

Go to BluSuite > Billing and select a new plan from the Available Plans section. Changes take effect immediately.
